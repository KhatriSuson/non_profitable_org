About Prakash Thapa Trust

Prakash Thapa Trust is a non-profit organization based in Nepal, dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged youth through education, training, and community outreach programs. Founded by Prakash Thapa in 2023, the trust has since been a beacon of hope for many young individuals.
